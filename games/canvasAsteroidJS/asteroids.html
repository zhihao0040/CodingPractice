Asteroids

How to play:

Use cursor to aim(adjust angle)

Hold /space/ to shoot in direction of the angle

Hold /w/ to move towards angle

Game only ends when you lose all your lives.

The score is calculated from the amount of time you stayed alive.

Sometimes asteroids which are big enough will burst into smaller green asteroids.

Smaller green asteroids will make more smaller green asteroids, usually found in level 2 or more.
